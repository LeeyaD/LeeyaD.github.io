Real-time as a Service

Twine's dedicated infrastructure abstracts away the complexity of maintaining WebSocket connections, so devs can focus on enhancing their app's business logic without getting bogged down in real-time implementation details.

Auto-scaling

Twine's Dockerized business logic runs within an auto-scaling AWS Elastic Beanstalk environment, with conservative and customizable scaling triggers.

Connection State Recovery

Users who experience a droppect connection can effortlessly resume the data stream – or connection state – from the exact point of interruption. .
